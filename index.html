We educate to help youth succeed in becoming life-long learners.

We develop our young people by effectively involving them in direct implementation of the core activities that develops their skills, enabling them become self-reliance and sustainable people in community service and development.

SOME OF O-YES FOCUS AREAS

Education

O-YES aims to empower the youth through teaching, experience & knowledge sharing platforms, trainings, workshops, seminars, and programs.

Youth Leadership

O-YES has designed youth leadership programs that aims at improving the leadership skills/abilities of the youth and enable them to be active leaders of the society.

Entrepreneurship, Creativity & Innovation

O-YES recognizes the importance of entrepreneurship and supporting youth to develop new ideas and to discover new ways of looking at problems and opportunities.

Youth Unemployment

O-YES committed and strive to seek and create productive employment, safe and acceptable work.

Youth Migration

O-YES strives to mitigate associated risks by contributing towards end illegal migration and committed to ensure that the benefits and opportunities of youth migration are fully harnessed.

Civic Engagement & Volunteering

O-YES focuses on four pillars namely, Civic Action, Civic Commitment, Civic Skills and Social Cohesion to develop programs & activities that combine knowledge, skills, values, and motivation of the youth to make this difference.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDG’s)

O-YES implements programs on SDG’s starting from awareness creation on the community to actual implementation of various development projects geared towards achieving the 17 goals.
